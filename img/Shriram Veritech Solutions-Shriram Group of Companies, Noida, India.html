    ABOUT VERITECH

    Ever since its inception in 1992, Shriram Veritech Solutions has focussed on innovation and customization in order to ensure customer delight. Intensive R&D along with working together with the clients has helped create a formidable range of products such as Holograms, Labels, Stamping Foils & many more that have been instrumental in protecting their brands by checking counterfeiting and pilferage. In the process, we have also engendered long lasting relationships by helping companies to increase their bottom lines while protecting their brand image.
